Fatal Police Encounters in the US

From Jan. 1, 2015 to Jul. 31, 2017, The Washington Post compiled a database of every fatal shooting in the US by a police officer in the line of duty. The Washington Post tracked more than a dozen details about each killing - including the race, age and gender of the deceased, whether the person was armed, among others. This information is displayed as a scatter plot of the dates of the fatal encounters against the states they occurred in. The legend is below the plot.
